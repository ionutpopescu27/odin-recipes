Kebab Recipe

So you found out where happiness comes from, finally.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onion, parsley, garlic, and salt and pepper. Form the mixture into 8 balls, and flatten into patties.
  3. Grill patties about 5 minutes on each side, until well done.
