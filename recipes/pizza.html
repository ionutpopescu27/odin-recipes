Pizza Recipe

Either an average "I'm hungry and don't want to spend a lot" pick, or you speak italian.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup onion

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread dough out on a lightly greased pizza pan.
  3. Spread sauce over dough, and top with cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, bell pepper, and onion.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
