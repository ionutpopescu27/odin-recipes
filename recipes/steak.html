Steak Recipe

Giga Chad (CBum) with a warrior's physique.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef steak
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and pepper. Place the steak in a shallow glass dish with the marinade, and turn to coat. For optimum flavor, rub the liquid into the meat. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours.
  2. Preheat grill for high heat.
  3. Brush grill grate with oil. Discard marinade, and place steak on the grill. Cook 7 minutes on each side, or to desired doneness.
